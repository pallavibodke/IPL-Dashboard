# IPL-Dashboard
An interactive and visually rich IPL Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze Indian Premier League match data. This dashboard provides insights into team performance, player statistics, tournament records, toss decisions, batting and bowling achievements, and overall season trends.
## Dataset used
-<a href="https://github.com/pallavibodke/IPL-Dashboard/blob/main/matches.csv" >Matches Dataset</a>
-<a href="https://github.com/pallavibodke/IPL-Dashboard/blob/main/deliveries.csv%20(1).zip" >deliveries Dataset</a>

## Question (Kips)
-Who won the IPL tournament season?
-Which player scored the highest runs (Orange Cap)?
-Which bowler took the highest wickets (Purple Cap)?
-What is the total number of runs scored in the tournament?
-How many total wickets were taken?
-How many 4’s and 6’s were hit overall?
-Which batsmen are in the Top 5 based on runs scored?
-Which bowlers are in the Top 5 based on wickets taken?
-Which teams won the most matches?
-What is the winning percentage based on toss decisions (bat/field)?
-Which player received the most Player of the Match awards?
-How do batting strike rate and bowling economy vary among players?
-Which season or year performed best using year-wise filtering?
-What are the comparative batting and bowling performances across teams and players?
