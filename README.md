# IPL-Dashboard
An interactive and visually rich IPL Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze Indian Premier League match data. This dashboard provides insights into team performance, player statistics, tournament records, toss decisions, batting and bowling achievements, and overall season trends.
## Dataset used
-<a href="https://github.com/pallavibodke/IPL-Dashboard/blob/main/matches.csv" >Matches Dataset</a>
-<a href="https://github.com/pallavibodke/IPL-Dashboard/blob/main/deliveries.csv%20(1).zip" >deliveries Dataset</a>

## Question (Kips)
-Who won the IPL tournament season?

-Which player scored the highest runs (Orange Cap)?

-Which bowler took the highest wickets (Purple Cap)?

-What is the total number of runs scored in the tournament?

-How many total wickets were taken?

-How many 4’s and 6’s were hit overall?

-Which batsmen are in the Top 5 based on runs scored?

-Which bowlers are in the Top 5 based on wickets taken?

-Which teams won the most matches?

-What is the winning percentage based on toss decisions (bat/field)?

-Which player received the most Player of the Match awards?

-How do batting strike rate and bowling economy vary among players?

-Which season or year performed best using year-wise filtering?

-What are the comparative batting and bowling performances across teams and players?

## Project Insights
Kolkata Knight Riders emerged as the tournament winner in the selected IPL season.
Virat Kohli was the highest run scorer and secured the Orange Cap.
HV Patel took the maximum wickets and earned the Purple Cap.
Teams choosing to field after winning the toss showed a higher winning percentage compared to batting first.
Boundary scoring (4’s and 6’s) played a major role in increasing team totals and match victories.
Players with higher strike rates had a strong impact on batting performance and match outcomes.
Bowling economy and strike rate were important indicators of effective bowling performance.
Top-performing teams maintained a balance between strong batting and disciplined bowling.
Player of the Match awards highlighted consistently impactful players across matches.

## Final Conclusion
The IPL Analysis Dashboard successfully transforms raw cricket data into meaningful insights using Power BI.
The project helps in understanding team strategies, player performances, and match-winning factors through 
interactive visualizations and KPIs. It demonstrates how data analytics can be effectively used in sports to 
evaluate performance trends, compare players and teams, and support better decision-making through clear and engaging dashboards.
